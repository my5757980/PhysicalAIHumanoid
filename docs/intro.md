Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This AI-native textbook covers the fundamental concepts of embodied intelligence and humanoid robotics, from basic principles to advanced applications.

## About This Textbook

This textbook provides a thorough exploration of Physical AI - an emerging field that combines artificial intelligence with physical systems to create machines that can perceive, reason, and act in the real world. Unlike traditional AI systems that operate primarily in digital spaces, Physical AI systems are embodied, meaning they have physical form and interact with their environment through sensors and actuators.
